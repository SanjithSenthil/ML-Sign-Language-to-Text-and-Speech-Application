Good communication is the bridge between confusion and clarity

The application has been developed to interpret American sign language (ASL), converting them to text and speech across five popular languages. This functionality serves to enhance communication for individuals with speech impairments and deafness. In addition, this innovation not only improves communication for ASL users but also offers them the chance to delve into and expand their understanding of the sign language. The supported languages include English, French, Chinese (Mandarin), Spanish, and Japanese.
